Acceptance: done means a new tester finishes all five onboarding screens unaided.

## Done Means

A new tester finishes all five onboarding screens without asking for help.

## Evidence Is

A screen recording of one tester completing it, plus the five screens committed to
the repo.

## Still Not Done If

Any screen needs a verbal explanation, or the tester stalls partway through.
